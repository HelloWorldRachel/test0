How well do you know your cars

Narrative Visualization Structure

The data set is an open source data that allows you explor the cars.

This narrative visualization follow slideshow hybrid structure, which mixes author-lead and reader-read structures.  There are two slides in total, during each slide, you can read the materials or you can just choose to conitune on if you are not interested in the details.

Scenes

I have two slides and the scene is demonstrated by slide two.  In this scene, I have the graph locating on the left with a coordinated set, and the the informations are on the right.

 

The scenes have been kept uniform in order to add a level of coherence for the user. The graph is placed on the left side of the screen with information regarding the scenes, parameters, annotations and triggers on the right. Please look out for the navigation markers on the right to proceed. Further information about the narrative visualization is on the next page.

What Next ?

Please click on the second unhighlighed badge ( number 2 in black) to your right to proceed

Presets

You can select one of the presets and click Add to Bucket to add it for analysis.If you add a preset to your bucket any existing country added will be removed.

Visualization Structure
Scenes
The data in this scene is for almost 200 countries. We try to contrast between the change in population living in rural areas between 1980 to 2014. The premise here is that a greater percentage change indicates greater urbanization which is an Indicator of development.
Annotations
The annotations here are the tooltips on each country. The tooltip gives information regarding the population in 1980 and 2014. It also gives you the population of the country that can help you contrast the population migration vs the actual population.
Parameters
The parameters that you can select in this chart are the countries, you can select the countries you want to analyse. Clicking a country adds it to your bucket to process for further analysis. You can also click on the button to take a deep dive into your selection.
Triggers
The triggers are that if you select a country it gets added to your bucket. If you select the a continent from the legend the corresponding countries also get highlighted.

Visualization Structure

Scenes
The data in this series of scenes is for the countries that you have selected from the screen above. In these scenes you will find a line graph showing the growth of every country over the years. In these scenes I have also added the values for Low, Middle and High Income countries. These act as hybrid annotations to provide a useful juxtaposition to judge the development of your selected countries.
Annotations
The annotations here are the tooltips for each year. Please hover over any of the years and you will see the actual data value for that year for your selected country. The tooltips are cleared once you move away from the selected year and line graph.
Parameters
The parameter here is the page number which you can select to shift between the various indicators. These are the page numbers displayed on top. You also have the page numbers on the right of your screen to change your country selection. You can also select any of the items from the legend to see it highlighted.
Triggers
Once you select a page number from the above displayed numbers you will be shifted to the next indicator. If at any moment you feel like changing your selection you are free to do so at which point you will be navigated back to the country selection page. On selecting a legend the corresponding data values are highlighted
